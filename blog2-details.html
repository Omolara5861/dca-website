There are a lot of roles you can pursue without having to code or have experience of coding. There are many industries that value skills beyond just coding. Here are some job roles that you can do;

  1. Project manager: they oversee different aspect of a project from planning, execution and monitoring. Also, strong leadership skill, organisational and communication.
  2. Digital marketing specialist:  these people promote products or service using online channels, this include social media, email marketing SEO and paid advertising. You need critical skills like creativity, data analysis, and strategic thinking
  3. UI/UX designer: User interface (ui) and user experience (ux) designers focus on creating Intuitive and user friendly digital interfaces. While some coding knowledge is helpful, it’s not always required.
  4. Financial analyst: they examine financial data and trends to provide recommendation for investment decision, strong analytical and math skills are required
  5. Business analyst: they analyze data and processes to identify business needs and propose solutions, involving implementation of technology
  6. Cyber security Analyst: they protect organization digital asserts by monitoring security threats and implementing security measures. you need problem solving skills
  7. IT project coordinator they assist in managing technology projects and ensure it’s in track, witching budget and meets the organizational goals.
  8. Technical Account Manager: they act as liaisons between tech companies and clients, to ensure clients needs are met and products are used effectively
  9. User experience (UX researcher): They study uses behaviour and preferences to inform the design of user friendly products
  10. Product support specialist: They assist customers with technical issues related to company’s product/services. You need communication and excellent problem solving skills
  11. Technical support specialist: They help end users troubleshoot and resolve software and hardware issues.
